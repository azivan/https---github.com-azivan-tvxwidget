Install the Widget

Log into Telavox Admin och go to Administration > Flow Widget > Create New Widget

Step 1

Name your live chat and add which agents, ie colleagues, will respond to chats. Also choose which phone number, e-mail address and opening hours should be visible.

When adding numbers, click "Change" to adjust what information should be displayed about the phone number - opening hours, number of people queue, and more.

Now you choose what color the widget should have and whether it should be to the right or left of your web page.

Here are lots of colors to choose from.

Enter the URL you want the widget to appear on and click "Save".

Telavox automatically creates the code / script tag that you or your web developer then paste into your website.
